Projects

Portfolio of My Proven Expertise

LeadScorify

  • Problem Solved: Real estate agencies often struggle to efficiently identify high-potential leads and investment hotspots from large datasets, leading to missed opportunities and inefficient resource allocation.
  • Approach: I developed "LeadScorify," a Streamlit-based ML platform that automates lead evaluation. The platform uses Random Forest and XGBoost models trained on over 20 engineered features to predict ROI and classify hotspots. Data imbalance was addressed using SMOTE.
  • Result: The platform boosted lead conversion targeting by over 30% by automatically ranking leads from CSV uploads. This work was presented at the IEEE CONNECT 2025 International conference.

Machine Learning | Predictive Analytics | Feature Engineering | SMOTE Analysis | Streamlit

FinBuddy

  • Problem Solved: Customers in the financial sector require immediate and accurate answers to complex queries about loans, EMIs, and credit, which traditional customer service channels can be slow to provide.
  • Approach: I developed "FinBuddy," an AI chatbot using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) + LLM architecture to provide reliable financial advice. The system's knowledge was grounded in BFSI datasets, and its conversational pipelines were optimized using Python, LangChain, and TensorFlow.
  • Result: FinBuddy successfully resolved over 1,000 user queries with a 92% accuracy rate. The optimized pipeline reduced query resolution time by 40%, providing users with instant, reliable support.

LLMs | RAG | LangChain | TensorFlow | Python
